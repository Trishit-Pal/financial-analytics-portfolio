üìä Key Metrics Explained

Total Return
+18.5%

Overall gain or loss over the selected period, including price appreciation.

Sharpe Ratio
1.24

Risk-adjusted return. Values >1 are good, >2 are excellent.

Volatility
18.5%

Annualized standard deviation of returns; higher values indicate more price fluctuation.

Alpha
+2.1%

Excess return vs. benchmark (e.g. SPY) after adjusting for risk (beta).

Beta
0.92

Sensitivity to market moves. <1: less volatile than market; >1: more volatile.

Max Drawdown
-22.3%

Largest peak-to-trough decline, capturing worst historical loss over the period.

VaR 95%
-3.5%

Historical Value at Risk: worst expected daily loss at 95% confidence.

Price-to-Earnings (P/E) and Earnings Per Share (EPS) for fundamentals-based stock selection.

Income & Size
